Distributed Energy Management Platform

Designing and leading a full-stack platform for cost-optimized battery operation and distributed energy infrastructure.

RoleSoftware Architect, Full-Stack Developer, Technical Lead
OrganizationMentorics GmbH
PeriodMay 2025 - Present
TeamTechnical lead for three developers

Overview

The platform monitors and controls distributed energy infrastructure and enables cost-optimized battery operation by using dynamic electricity prices. It connects physical devices at local sites with edge software, central backend services, operational tooling, and web applications.

My Role

I designed the distributed full-stack architecture and implement core components across the platform. My responsibility spans technical direction, hands-on development, infrastructure, production deployments, and coordination of the development team.

  • Designed component boundaries and communication flows across central backend services, edge software, and Raspberry Pi-based device controllers.
  • Developed backend services, REST APIs, WebSocket and MQTT communication, authentication, and time-series data processing.
  • Built React-based dashboards and management interfaces for energy data, device status, and administration.
  • Established GitHub Actions CI/CD, Docker environments, release workflows, VPN connectivity, monitoring, and secure certificate-based communication.
  • Lead three developers and coordinate implementation, reviews, testing, documentation, and technical decisions.
