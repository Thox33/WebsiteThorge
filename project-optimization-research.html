Optimization Research

Applying integer optimization and incremental MaxSAT to complex real-time decision problems.

DegreeMaster of Science in Computer Science
UniversityHamburg University of Technology
FocusIncremental MaxSAT and multi-objective optimization

Research Objective

My Master's thesis investigated incremental MaxSAT for multi-objective optimization in real-time taxi-sharing services. The work addressed how complex and changing optimization problems can be modeled and solved efficiently under time-sensitive conditions.

My Work

  • Analyzed a complex real-time optimization problem and translated it into suitable formal models.
  • Investigated incremental MaxSAT approaches for changing multi-objective constraints.
  • Designed experiments, evaluated results, and communicated technical conclusions clearly.
  • Connected theoretical optimization methods with a practical mobility use case.

Technology & Methods

Core methods

Incremental MaxSAT, integer optimization, multi-objective optimization, algorithm evaluation

Transferable strengths

Analytical problem solving, formal modeling, experimental design, technical writing
